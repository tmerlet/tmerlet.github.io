PROJECT 1

Create a course booking system for General assembly

I used Ruby on Rails, HTML5 and CSS3 to to build my first ever app!

After 4 weeks of lessons, it was great to put some of what we learnt into practice. I had to choose one of three projects, and like this one as I could focus on Rails, Database models and presentation. I based the look of this app on the actual General assembly website in order to maintain the company image.


I wanted to use a visual calendar, but since we had not learned how to do this on the course, I went out and found the 'event calendar' gem which helped me to do this, which meant I was the only scheduling app with a calendar. Unfortunalty this gem prevents me from now uploading to heroku, but the code is available online, or I would love to demo the app for you on local host!
