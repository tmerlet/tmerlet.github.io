PROJECT 1

Create a course booking system for General assembly

I used Ruby on Rails, HTML5 and CSS3 to to build my first ever app!

I had to choose one of three projects, and chose this one as I could focus on Rails, Database models and CSS. I based the appearance of this app on the actual General assembly website in order to maintain the company image.


I wanted to use a visual calendar, but since this was not something we had covered in class, I had to find out how to do this online. I found the 'event calendar' gem which helped me to do this. Despite the fact that a few of us had chosen to do this project, I was the only one with a visual calendar. You can view this by clicking on the red video presentation link on the right hand side.
